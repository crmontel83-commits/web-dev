How the Payment Plan Works

Clear, simple terms — no surprise invoices, no hidden add-ons.

Project Fee

The total project fee for a standard website build is $995. This covers strategy, design, development, basic integrations, and launch support for one site.

Monthly Payments

You have the option to pay this fee in monthly installments until the full $995 is reached. Once your payment plan is set up, you can continue paying each month until the total is complete.

Non-Refundable Payments

All payments made toward the $995 project fee are considered a non-refundable deposit. These payments reserve space in my schedule and cover project preparation, communication, and planning time.

I begin design and development work on your project after the full $995 has been paid.

If you decide to cancel or pause before the full amount is paid, any payments already made will be retained and will not be refunded. In some cases, at my discretion, your paid amount may be applied as a credit toward a future service within 12 months.

By starting a payment plan, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.
